Fish Cakes

Description

The following is a Fisch cakes recipe.

Fisch cakes have been a staple in my household. Especially during those days when we need eat well on a budget. With basically just a can of tuna, you can have a delicious meal!

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of tuna
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 green onions, diced
  • 1/2 onion, cut into strips
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients, except for the breadcrumbs, in a large bowl and mash until thoroughly combined.
  2. Form the mixture into 8 equal patties and roll them in the panko breadcrumbs. Refrigerate the patties for at least 30 minutes or until they've firmed up.
  3. Heat some oil in a non-stick pan and fry the patties on each side for 3-4 minutes until lightly browned, being careful not to overcrowd the pan.
  4. Serve immediately. Taste great with tarter sauce or ketchup!
